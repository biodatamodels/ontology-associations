Class: gaf association

line of GAF

URI: ontology_association:GafAssociation

Parents

  • is_a: XafAssociation - line of xAF. xAF is a generalization of GAF designed to work for other ontologies than GO, and for other entities beyond genes and proteins. xAF has the same column headers to GAF, but has fewer restrictions.

Uses Mixin

  • mixin: Denormalized - mixin for a denormalized class. A denormalized class is formed from a join of two or more other classes
  • mixin: GoRelated - mixin for any association that is GO related
